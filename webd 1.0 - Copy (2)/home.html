About the Watch Dogs Series

The Watch Dogs series is an action-adventure video game franchise developed and published by Ubisoft. The series explores the concept of hacking and the impact of technology on society, placing players in the role of skilled hackers navigating complex and interconnected cities.

Key features of the series include:

  • Hacking Gameplay: Players use hacking abilities to manipulate the city's infrastructure, control electronic devices, and gain an advantage over enemies.
  • Open-World Exploration: Each installment features a detailed open-world environment that players can explore, complete missions, and interact with various characters.
  • Compelling Narratives: The series tells immersive stories with themes of surveillance, privacy, and the consequences of unchecked technological power.

Whether it's the original Watch Dogs, the sunny landscapes of Watch Dogs 2, or the dystopian near-future setting of Watch Dogs: Legion, the series offers a unique blend of hacking, stealth, and action elements.
